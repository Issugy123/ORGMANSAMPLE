Our Bubble Story

Creating joyful, cute, and energetic makeup for everyone since 2018.

How It All Started

Bubble Cosmetics was born from a simple idea: makeup should be fun, expressive, and accessible to everyone. Our founder, Emma Chen, was frustrated with the serious, exclusive nature of the beauty industry.

In 2018, Emma started creating vibrant, playful cosmetics in her apartment kitchen. She wanted to create products that would bring joy to people's daily routines and help them express their unique personalities.

What began as a small passion project quickly grew into a beloved brand known for its joyful approach to beauty, cute packaging, and high-quality formulations that work for everyone.

Today, Bubble Cosmetics continues to spread joy and confidence through our products, staying true to our mission of making beauty fun and accessible for all.

Our Policies

The standards we uphold in our business practices.

Sustainability Commitment

At Bubble Cosmetics, we're committed to reducing our environmental impact. We use recyclable packaging, sustainably sourced ingredients, and continuously work to minimize waste in our production process.

Our goal is to be carbon neutral by 2025, and we're investing in renewable energy and offset programs to achieve this target.

Ethical Standards

We believe in ethical business practices at every level. This includes fair wages for all employees, transparent supply chains, and cruelty-free product development.

We never test on animals and work only with suppliers who share our commitment to ethical practices and human rights.
